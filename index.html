Anime Arcade

Test your anime knowledge by playing a traditional memory match game! However, there's a slight twist. Instead of matching images, you will be matching the anime name with the anime image! Select from 3 different difficulties and try to complete it in time!

Want a classic way of proving your anime knowledge? Guess the anime name based on some information based on related information such as genres, the myAnimeList score, and more! You will have 1 minute to try and guess as much anime as you can!

Everyone knows about the newly popular game Wordle! The twist in this game is that the words will be 5 letter anime character names instead of traditional 5 letter words. Test your knowledge of your favorite characters in this fun twist of Wordle!
